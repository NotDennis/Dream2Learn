Our Mission


At Dream2Learn, we strive to help low-income students as equally as others to succeed in their education.

We have programs and activities that are designed to be free, effective, and efficient that will help our students to reach their goals and fulfill their potential in academia.

Facts and Questions

Concerns? Look to see if we have already answered your question. Question not listed? Use our contact page below.



Who and what are our qualifications?

We are high school student volunteers that are willing to help you learn and succeed. All of us are AP students that have a good understanding of the subjects and topics and are able to educate others.

What can I get help with?

We have different tutors that can help you in various subjects. If you are struggling in English, Science, Social Studies, or Math; we have the ability to help.

Please note that it would be best if you send us your material such as the textbooks, homework, or notes prior to the tutoring session so our tutors can preview the material and give you a better understanding of the topics.

How do I get a tutor?

You can get a tutor by signing up in the sign-up page, or alternatively contact us through the contact page and send us your message. Both work fine and we are happy to help you.

Can I work with the same tutor again?

Absolutely, we schedule multiple sessions with your tutor even if it is your first time to sign up.

How often can I work with my tutor?

Our tutors are available once, twice, or three times a week based on his/her schedule. We do not have a limit on how often you can work with a tutor.

What's the minimum time to work with a tutor?

Tutoring session time is between 30 minutes to an hour based on your choice. Please go to the sign-up page if you are interested.
